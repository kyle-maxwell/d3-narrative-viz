China's Demography

Below is China's demography in 2020. Due to the one-child policy, an unsuprising drop in the number of children can be seen. In conjunction with the drop in fetility rates due to modernization, the country is experiencing insufficient young parents to repopulate. This means the Chinese consumption boom is at its peak too; there will be fewer future consumers.

Additionally, China is strongly patriarchal thus the one-child policy resulted in a large degree of sex-selective abortion. This is resulting in a massive male-female imbalance which feeds back into less couples and smaller future generations.
